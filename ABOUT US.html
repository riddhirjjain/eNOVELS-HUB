ABOUT US

eNOVELS HUB was established with the vision to provide an extensive library of books in digital format for free on the Internet. Many of the early eBooks are from the obooko archives, which means you will be able to find a lot of classics on the site.
We are still going strong in our mission to provide a valuable service to our readers. eNOVELS HUB has grown into a platform where self-publishing authors have the opportunity to introduce their work to our community and new books are uploaded to our site daily - provided they comply to our quality standards.
